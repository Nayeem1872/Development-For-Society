About Our Organization

Development For Society is a non-government organization committed to the economic and social empowerment of disadvantaged people across Bangladesh.

It’s our pleasure to note that the organization has grown up and reached its adolescence withdiversity of programs and it achieved notable success in program development and management.What makes us different is that the program has been developed through the learning process indirectly working with the community people. The program has evolved through the activeparticipation and through a consultative process with the community people in identifying theirneeds, hopes and aspiration. .

We are influenced by a vision to work with the people to liberate them from hunger, disease, ignorance and oppression and facilitate them to move ahead towards life, solidarity, integrity, love and decent living. Our role is to catalyst the social movement and action of the people so that they can visualize and dream about a more human future beyond the fractured and struggled present. In working with the poor for a decade, Development for Society has specialized on poverty reduction strategies, non-formal and formal primary schooling, women and child empowerment, reproductive health services, human resource development and promotion of agriculture and micro-credit. There is no denying that with decade of long experience, we have learnt to motivate, organize and empower the poor, the destitute and the most vulnerable sections of the community through social awareness, education, and training and local resource mobilization. We are forwarding march with pro-poor and pro-women campaign and advocacy program for sustainable development of the community. It is worth mentioning to note that we are operating social and economic empowerment activities in 245 villages of 12 Upazilas under four districts covering more than 425,125 direct beneficiaries. Moreover, our strategies are concentrated in providing reproductive health services to the poor, extending training for skill development, promoting fisheries and agricultural input delivery, creating credit facilities along with necessary technical supports to the disadvantaged segment of the population in the community including slum dwellers. Additionally, entrepreneurship development program has been encouraged for the small entrepreneurs. We have also been successful to offer Credit plus Services to the valued group members of our Society. They are linked with other essential services like general & reproductive health services, training and agricultural input delivery. Our micro- finance program is an alternative to the formal banking system for the poor. The recovery rate is extremely high and it is almost 100% in case of women borrowers. We are well prepared to increase loan portfolio in our microfinance and micro-enterprise development programs to accelerate employment creation for the poor and the marginalized groups.

Our Mission

Development for Society is an association of individuals, who have deep concern and social commitment for public cause and interest. It has been dreaming about more human future for women and children cause and interest too. It came in to being in 1996 in response to the widely felt needs of the most vulnerable and disadvantaged sections of the community.

Our Vision

In addition to poverty reduction, it has proven its excellence in creating an opportunity for education to the children of the poor and the most destitute families particularly, the street children, working children and adolescent boys and girls, who either have never been enrolled in a formal school or have been dropped out from the formal schooling. It has also been moving ahead for women empowerment

About Us
