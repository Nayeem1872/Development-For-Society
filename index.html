About Our Organization

Development For Society is a non-government organization committed to the economic and social empowerment of disadvantaged people across Bangladesh.

It believes in realizing the potential of men and women to become strong, independent members of their communities. .

Development for Society its mission with strategically expanded its service network to reach remote rural areas. Over the years, it has widened its range of development services to include basic health care and education, winter clothes, solar power, skills training and many other things. Development for Society was founded in July 1996 and now serves almost 500,000 households within 6 brunches of Bangladesh.

Our Mission

Development for Society aims at promoting a participatory approach to actions and reflections that improves education and health, ensures sustainable livelihood, promotes human rights and good governance and builds institutions and networks of the poor..

Our Vision

We adopt non-directive, non-paternalistic and bottom up and participatory development approach to community empowerment. It is highly people centered development approach. It stresses on people’s participation in need assessment, project planning, implementation, monitoring and decision making process of the community people and other stakeholders at all level.

Our Achievemtns

Development for Society compacting human development, disaster management of Bangladesh. We are also participating in our activities around human rights, social justice, and other social supporting works.
